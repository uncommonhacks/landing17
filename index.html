About

Hack uncommon. Think uncommon. Be uncommon.

We're a student group at the University of Chicago that seeks to build community and encourage participation in tech by running a hackathon and other events at UChicago.

We run an annual intercollegiate hackathon, which will be held February 10-11, 2018.

Additionally, we're organizing Uncommon Makes, a one-day event on November 18, 2017 for UChicago students to learn and build with techology.
